About

Digital Hub represents an open source technology platform integrating various software components for managing, elaborating and exposing data and services in a standardized, aligned manner. It is built upon a wide range of open source projects and open standards.

Digital Hub Platform is developed and maintained by Co-Innovation Lab, a joint initiative of Dedagroup and Fondazione Bruno Kessler dedicated to developing standards and best practices for availability and interoperability of data and services (Open Data, Open Services) and to develop new-generation digital applications.
